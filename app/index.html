Box Breathing

Box breathing is a very simple technique that can ease anxiety and lower your heart rate.

Sit with your back straight, feet flat on the floor, with your hands resting in your lap.

Breathe in through your nose as the box fills up. Hold your breath as it spins. exhale through your mouth as it empties. Hold your breath once more as it spins. Repeat.
